About Celestial Self

What This Is

Celestial Self is a free collection of divination tools — numerology, tarot, and astrology — built for every identity.

Most divination sites assume you're straight. The love readings default to "he" and "she." The compatibility guides imagine one kind of relationship. The tarot interpretations center one kind of experience. We thought that was worth fixing.

Our tools are designed from the ground up to be gender-neutral, identity-affirming, and free of heteronormative assumptions. Whether you're queer, trans, non-binary, questioning, or simply tired of divination that wasn't built with you in mind — this is your space.

What This Isn't

This is not a psychic service. We don't predict your future, and we don't pretend the stars are making your decisions. There is no "premium reading" behind a paywall. No one here is channeling spirits or guaranteeing outcomes.

We also aren't here to tell you what your identity means or how your relationships should work. The tools present ancient frameworks. You decide what resonates. Your experience is the authority — not ours.

Our tagline says it all: None of this is real. All of it is useful.

Our Values

  • Inclusive by design, not by afterthought. Every tool, every reading, every word on this site was built without gendered assumptions. This isn't a standard site with a rainbow flag added on top — inclusivity is the foundation.
  • Your identity is yours. We don't define you. We don't assume who you love, how you identify, or what your life looks like. The tools are mirrors — you decide what the reflection means.
  • Honesty over mystique. We'd rather be upfront about what divination can and can't do than pretend we have powers we don't.
  • Beauty matters. If you're going to spend time reflecting on yourself, the space you do it in should feel good.
  • Free means free. No sign-up, no email capture, no hidden upsells. The tools work without giving us anything.
  • Privacy is non-negotiable. We use Google Analytics to understand traffic patterns. We don't collect, store, or sell any personal data you enter into our tools. Your birth date stays in your browser.

Who Made This

Celestial Self was created by an independent team who noticed something obvious: the divination world was built for one kind of person, and everyone else was expected to translate. We wanted to build the site we wished existed — one where the tools, the language, and the design honor every way of being human.

We're not here to speak for any community. We're here to build tools that don't exclude anyone. If we get something wrong, we want to know — reach out through our contact page.

None of this is real. All of it is useful.
