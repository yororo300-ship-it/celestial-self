Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The Short Version

We respect your privacy. We don't collect your personal data. Any information you enter into our tools (birth dates, etc.) is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers. We use Google Analytics to understand how people use this site. That's it.

Information We Collect

Personal data you enter into tools: None of it is collected, stored, transmitted, or shared. Your birth date and any other information you enter into our numerology, tarot, and astrology tools stays entirely in your browser. We never see it.

Analytics data: We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous usage data such as pages visited, time spent on site, approximate geographic location (country/city level), device type and browser, and referral source. This helps us understand which tools are popular and how to improve the site. Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. No personally identifiable information is collected through Analytics.

Cookies

This site uses cookies only through Google Analytics. These cookies are used to distinguish unique visitors and understand usage patterns. They do not contain any personal information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to analyze site traffic. Google may process data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Vercel: This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect server logs including IP addresses as part of standard web hosting. See Vercel's privacy policy for details.

We may display third-party advertisements in the future. If and when we do, this privacy policy will be updated to reflect the advertising services used and the data they collect.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to third parties. Period.

Children's Privacy

This site is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided data through this site, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to request, modify, or delete. If you have questions or concerns about your privacy on this site, please reach out through our contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
